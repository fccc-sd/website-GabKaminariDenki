Welcome to Fall!

Hello, and welcome to the Fall facts page! Here you can get to know some facts about Fall that you may not have known before.

Facts About Fall

1. In the Northern Hemisphere, fall starts in September and ends in December, while in the Southern Hemisphere, fall begins in March and ends in June.

2. The first day of fall is determined by the date of the autumnal equinox, which varies from year to year.

3. The Earth has two equinoxes each year, one in spring and one in fall.

4. During the fall months, the Northern Hemisphere tilts farther and farther away from the sun.

5. Poets often described this season using the phrase “the fall of the leaves.”
