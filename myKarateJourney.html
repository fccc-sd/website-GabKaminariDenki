My Karate Journey

I have always wanted to do karate since I was little. My Dad had the Karate Kid movies that came when he was a kid/teenager. I have been doing karate for two years now and I just gotten my yellow belt which is a big step in the style of karate I do. The style of karate I do is Shorinryu-Shorinkan. It's a mouth full to say, so I ask people if they seen the Karate Kid movies or seen the Cobra Kai series, and if they have I tell them I do MiyagiDo.
